Google launched the TPU Developer Hub, a centralized educational resource designed to help model builders, optimizers, and developers unlock the full performance of Google Cloud TPUs. The hub provides code-first resources, open-source recipes, and documentation covering architecture, optimization, debugging, parallelism, and networking for both human developers and AI tools.
