This post introduces three architectural patterns that integrate Model Context Protocol (MCP) Apps with Agent-to-User Interface (A2UI), addressing the trade-off between iframe-based customization and secure native declarative rendering. The approaches let developers deliver secure, performant, and brand-consistent agentic experiences.
