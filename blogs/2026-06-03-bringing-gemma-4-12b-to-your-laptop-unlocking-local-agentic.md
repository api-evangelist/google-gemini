Google released Gemma 4 12B for local deployment on standard consumer laptops using Google AI Edge tools. Three key applications are covered: Google AI Edge Gallery for local data analysis and coding, Google AI Edge Eloquent for on-device voice dictation, and LiteRT-LM for serving local industry-compatible endpoints compatible with existing AI frameworks — all processing data locally without cloud connectivity.
